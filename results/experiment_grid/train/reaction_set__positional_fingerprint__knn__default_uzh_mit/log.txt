======================================================================
EXPERIMENT LOG
======================================================================

'Aggregation' means the peptide clumped together during synthesis
instead of staying dissolved -- something chemists want to predict
and avoid. This experiment checks how well a model can predict, from
the peptide and its synthesis conditions, whether aggregation happens.

Data split:              reaction_set
  -> Looking at every step of building each peptide, one amino acid at a time.

Data representation:    positional_fingerprint
  -> Like 'fingerprint', but gives more weight to amino acids added earlier during synthesis.

Prediction model:        knn
  -> K-Nearest Neighbours -- predicts based on the most similar peptides seen before.

Protecting-group scheme: default_uzh_mit
